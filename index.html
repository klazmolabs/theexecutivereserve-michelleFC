This service is delivered within a fully self-contained mobile styling station designed for corporate settings. Hair is lightly misted to allow controlled execution before a tailored cut is performed using high-performance tools.

Before completion, the edges are precisely refined through meticulous perimeter detailing, and the neck is finished with a cooling astringent. Hair is then professionally dried and styled with premium products, and all debris is fully vacuumed and cleared - leaving a clean, polished finish with true salon-level results.

Founder, The Executive Reserve

With over two decades of experience in New York’s leading salons, including Pierre Michel Salon and Davide Torchie Salon, I am known for clean, modern cuts and a meticulous, detail-driven approach.

My work is rooted in a family lineage of Portuguese barbers dating back to the 1920s. I operate privately and do not advertise, building long-standing client relationships through trust and discretion.

The Executive Reserve was created after years of serving clientele with demanding schedules and limited time. I saw a need for hair services integrated directly into the workday, eliminating travel and saving time. This evolved into an on-site program for individuals who value efficiency, consistency, and a high standard of personal presentation.
