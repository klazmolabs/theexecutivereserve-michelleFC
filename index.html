No Tipping Culture

This service is delivered within a fully self-contained, compact mobile styling station designed for corporate environments. Hair is lightly misted to ensure precision and control before a tailored men's cut is performed using high-performance cutting tools.

Prior to completion, the perimeter is meticulously refined, and the neck is thoroughly cleaned and finished with a cooling astringent. Hair is then professionally dried and styled with premium products. Any hair in contact with the skin is gently removed to prevent irritation, and all debris is fully vacuumed and cleared - resulting in a clean, polished finish with true salon-level results.
