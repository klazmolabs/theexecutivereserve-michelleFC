This service is delivered within a fully self-contained mobile styling station designed for corporate settings. Hair is lightly misted to allow controlled execution before a tailored cut is performed using high-performance tools.

Before completion, the edges are precisely refined through meticulous perimeter detailing, and the neck is finished with a cooling astringent. Hair is then professionally dried and styled with premium products, and all debris is fully vacuumed and cleared - leaving a clean, polished finish with true salon-level results.

Michelle Ferreira-Calisto

Principal, The Executive Reserve

With over two decades of experience in New York's leading salons, including Pierre Michel Salon and Davide Torchio Salon, I am known for clean, modern cuts and a meticulous, detail-driven approach. My work is rooted in a family lineage of Portuguese barbers dating back to the 1920s.

I operate privately and do not advertise, building long-standing client relationships through trust and discretion.

The Executive Reserve was created after years of serving clients with demanding schedules and limited time. I identified a need for hair services integrated directly into the workday-eliminating travel and maximizing efficiency. This vision evolved into an on-site program designed for individuals who value consistency, discretion, and a high standard of personal presentation.
